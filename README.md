This **Supply Chain Analytics Tableau Project** was developed to analyze **product performance, supply chain efficiency, manufacturing effectiveness, logistics costs, and customer demographics** for a **Fashion & Beauty (Makeup) startup**.

The project is built as a **Tableau Story** consisting of **five analytical dashboards**, each designed to answer a specific business question and guide stakeholders through a clear, insight-driven narrative.
